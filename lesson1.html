Computer servicing involves checking, cleaning, testing, and repairing different parts of a computer system to make sure it works efficiently. It helps prevent major damage and keeps the system running smoothly.

Regular maintenance includes cleaning dust from components, checking cables and connections, testing the power supply, and updating the operating system and software.
